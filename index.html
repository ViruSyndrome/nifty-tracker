Frequently Asked Questions

How often is the market data updated?
During market hours (9:15 AM – 3:30 PM IST, Monday to Friday), live indices and stock prices refresh automatically every 20 seconds. Global cues and commodity prices are updated throughout the day. Institutional (FII/DII) data is updated once daily, usually by 7:30 PM IST after NSE releases the final figures.
How is the Gold and Silver price calculated?
We use international spot prices (GC=F and SI=F) converted to INR using live USD/INR exchange rates, adjusted for India's import duties (6%) and GST (3%). This provides an indicative price for 10 grams of 24K/22K Gold and 10 grams of Silver, closely matching local IBJA rates.
What is FII and DII activity?
FII (Foreign Institutional Investors) and DII (Domestic Institutional Investors like Mutual Funds) are the major participants in the market. Tracking their net buy/sell value helps retail investors understand institutional sentiment and liquidity trends.
