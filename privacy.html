Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 8, 2026

GetNiftyReady ("we", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. Our core design philosophy is that your financial data should remain yours. We do not require accounts, sign-ups, or personal information to use our platform.

1. Zero Personal Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or sell any personally identifiable information (PII). Your stock searches, calculator inputs, and watchlists are processed locally in your browser. Our servers do not log your search queries.

2. Browser-Based Processing

All data fetching from financial APIs happens directly from your device. We use a proxy layer (Cloudflare Workers) only to facilitate the API requests, and no user-specific data is persisted during this process.

3. Analytics & Advertising

We use Google Analytics 4 to track anonymous, aggregate traffic patterns (visit counts, device types). This helps us improve the site. We also display ads via Google AdSense. These third-party services may use cookies to serve ads or track anonymous usage. You can manage your preferences via your browser settings or Google's ad settings.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at hello@getniftyready.com.
