About GetNiftyReady

GetNiftyReady is a free, no-signup Indian stock market tracker. We show live NSE and BSE share prices, Nifty 50 index, Sensex, Bank Nifty, top gainers, and top losers — all in one place.

What we offer

  • Live Nifty 50, Sensex, Bank Nifty, Nifty IT, Midcap & SGX Nifty indices
  • Search any NSE or BSE listed stock by name or ticker
  • Price, % change, 52-week high/low, market cap, volume
  • Top gainers and losers from Nifty 50
  • Auto-refreshes every 30 seconds during market hours

Data source

Data is sourced from Yahoo Finance via public APIs. Prices may be delayed up to 15 minutes. This tool is for informational purposes only and does not constitute financial advice.

Privacy

No personal data is collected. All stock data is fetched directly in your browser. See our Privacy Policy for details.
